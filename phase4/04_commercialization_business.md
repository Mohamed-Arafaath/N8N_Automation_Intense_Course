### üìä Pricing Frameworks

#### 1. **Value-Based Pricing** (Recommended)

**Formula**: Price = % of Value Created

**Example**:
```
Client: Law firm
Problem: Manually processing 100 contracts/month
Time spent: 40 hours/month
Cost: $4,000/month (paralegal at $100/hr)

Your Solution: AI contract processor
Time saved: 35 hours/month
Value: $3,500/month

Your Price: $1,500/month (43% of value)
Client Saves: $2,000/month
ROI: 2.3x
```

**Pricing Rule**: Charge 30-50% of value created

---

#### 2. **Time-Based Pricing**

**Your Hourly Rate**: $100-300/hour (depending on expertise)

**Example**:
```
Project: Lead generation system
Estimated Time: 20 hours
Your Rate: $150/hour
Price: $3,000

Add Buffer: +20% for revisions
Final Price: $3,600
```

**Pros**: Simple, predictable
**Cons**: Penalizes efficiency (faster = less money)

---

#### 3. **Performance Pricing**

**Model**: Base fee + performance bonus

**Example**:
```
Service: Lead generation system
Base Fee: $2,000 (setup)
Performance: $50 per qualified lead generated

Month 1: 40 leads = $2,000
Month 2: 60 leads = $3,000
Month 3: 80 leads = $4,000
```

**Pros**: Aligned incentives, high upside
**Cons**: Risk if system underperforms

---

#### 4. **Tiered Pricing**

**Example: Social Media Automation**

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|---------|-----|------------|
| Price | $500/mo | $1,500/mo | $3,000/mo |
| Platforms | 2 | 5 | Unlimited |
| Posts/Month | 30 | 100 | Unlimited |
| AI Content | ‚ùå | ‚úÖ | ‚úÖ |
| Video Generation | ‚ùå | ‚ùå | ‚úÖ |
| Support | Email | Slack | Dedicated |

**Strategy**: Most choose middle tier (anchoring effect)
